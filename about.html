Who am I?

I'm Natalia, I was born in Colombia and since I was little I love creating things with my hands, they are my best tools to materialize my ideas. I enjoy building with different materials, I love painting, woodworking, creating my jewelry, knitting and designing real and digital products

I am an empathetic person who enjoys putting myself in other people's shoes and seeing things from different perspectives. I have always had a passion for learning, whether it involves acquiring new skills, exploring hobbies, or studying languages. Currently, I am in my second year of the Interaction Design program at George Brown College (GBC), where I have gained extensive knowledge about virtual platform design. This program builds on my previous education as a Product Design Engineer at Eafit University in Colombia. I am proficient in various software applications, including Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Maya, 3D Studio Max, Substance 3D Painter, Creo CAD, Microsoft Office, Figma, Miro, Visual Studio Code, and more.
